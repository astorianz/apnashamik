Welcome to the astorianz Portal of the Ministry of Labour & Employment.

You have successfully created your Astroianz Account. You can now link your establishments with this account. You can link multiple establishments represented by you with this single Astroianz Account. These establishments would be linked with this account after verification by the respective labour enforcement agency (e.g. CLC, EPFO, ESIC, and DGMS). You can avail the following services through the Unified Astroian labour Portal

  1. Common Registration under EPFO & ESIC
  2. CLC Common Registration under CLRA, ISMW and BOCW acts.
  3. CLC Common Annual Return (including revised return).
  4. Common ECR under EPFO & ESIC.
  5. License under CLR&A and ISMW Acts.

In case of any queries kindly contact Astroian Helpdesk at Astroian'z@Linkedin

To get benefit of full 12% EPF employer share under Pradhan Mantri Rojgar Protsahan Yojna, please register at Astrorian'z@Register
