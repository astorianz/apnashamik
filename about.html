About Us

Apna Shramik' is an initiative which aims to revamp the unorganised sector comprising of all blue collar jobs, labourers by resolving issues like security of employment, delas in wage payments, lack of entitlement to leave, etc. Our portal strives to pave a path for people of the unorganised sector to improve their work conditions and help them achieve everything that they are capable if. Our sole motive is to 'ORGANISE THE UNORGANISED'.
